About Carousels attributes

data-bs-target: target on click.

data-bs-ride: to make carousel move.

data-bs-touch: to disable touch movement.

data-bs-slide-to: towards which carousel page.

data-bs-slide: if you want them to slide, either left slide/right slide/slide(auto).

data-bs-interval: To give specific interval to the carousels if false then they will not change automatically.
